Introduction

Hi Amigos!!!! Como Estas

GAURAV KESWANI is a Practising Company Secretary and an Associate Member of Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) since 2015. He is the founder of LEGALPRENEURS, a Professional Services Firm.

Mr. Keswani is a dynamic speaker and visiting faculty for various training programs held by Northern Region of ICSI. He was also the speaker of career awareness program conducted by Northern Region of ICSI in 2018. He energizes people to meet the challenges around them and aims to improve the life of many.

In addition to that he is a fitness lover and enthusiast especially calisthenics and inspiring people to get a healthy life.
