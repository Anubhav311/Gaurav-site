about

Gaurav Keswani is amongst millions, who struggles, falls and rises like an ordinary human; but his ‘never give-up’ attitude and persistent urge to improvise has helped him to achieve a better and focused life. His constant journey of self-development inspires him to join hands and benefit people by sharing his knowledge and experience to make the world a better place.

A practising Company Secretary and a member of ICSI (Institute of Company Secretaries of India), Gaurav is also the founder of GAURAV KESWANI & ASSOCIATES , a business and legal solutions firm.

He is a visiting faculty for various training programs held by Northern Region of ICSI, and also a spokesperson for career awareness programs held in several schools conducted by NIRC-ICSI. Being a dynamic speaker, he strengthens the self-confidence of people, which encourages them to overcome challenges and shape their personality.

Gaurav is a fitness enthusiast, especially calisthenics. He loves motivating people around him to adapt a healthy lifestyle to enrich their persona.



WHAT I WANNA ACHIEVE

Benefit people by sharing my knowledge, skills and experience for contributing towards society.



HOW I THINK IT WILL WORK

We can do this by operating knowledge sharing programs and by reaching the number of people it can reach.
